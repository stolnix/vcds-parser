# VCDS Parser

VCDS Parser is a simple utility app for working with VCDS `.txt` diagnostic logs.

## Overview

VCDS Parser allows users to open, parse, view, and search through VCDS diagnostic text logs directly on their device.

The app is designed for users who work with vehicle diagnostic logs and want a faster way to inspect the contents of VCDS text files.

## Features

* Open VCDS `.txt` diagnostic log files
* Parse diagnostic text logs into a more readable format
* Search through log contents
* Review diagnostic information locally on the device
* No account required
* No tracking
* No analytics
* No data collection

## Privacy

VCDS Parser processes files locally on the user’s device.

The app does not collect, upload, transmit, sell, or share user data.

## Intended Use

VCDS Parser is intended as a log viewing and search utility. It does not modify vehicles, perform diagnostics by itself, connect to vehicles, or replace professional diagnostic tools.
